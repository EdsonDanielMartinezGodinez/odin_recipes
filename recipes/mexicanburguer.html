Mexican Burguer

Description

These hamburguesas Mexicanas (Mexican hamburgers) are the best you'll ever have!

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds ground beef
  • 1 head iceberg lettuce, cored and shredded
  • 3 onions, minced, divided
  • 1 pound shredded pepper Jack cheese
  • 1 Â½ cups bread crumbs
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped and juices strained
  • 2 bunches cilantro, chopped
  • 3 limes, juiced, divided
  • 1 serrano chile pepper, minced, divided
  • 5 avocados, peeled and pitted
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 12 hamburger buns

How to make a mexican burguer step-by-step

  1. Combine ground beef, 1/2 onions, pepper Jack cheese, bread crumbs, and eggs in a bowl; form into 12 patties.
  2. Combine tomatoes, 1/2 remaining onion, cilantro, 1/2 lime juice, and 1/2 serrano pepper in a bowl; stir pico de gallo well. Cover and refrigerate to chill.
  3. Mash avocados, remaining onions, remaining lime juice, and remaining serrano pepper together in a bowl; season guacamole with salt and black pepper.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Add patties; cook to desired doneness, 3 to 5 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into centers should read 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
  5. Place 1 burger on each bun; top with guacamole, shredded lettuce, and pico de gallo.
